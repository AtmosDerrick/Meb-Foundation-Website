What is Blindness?

Blindness is a lack of vision.
It is the state or condition of being unable to see because of injury, diseases, or a congenital condition.

Types of Blindness?

If you're partially blind, you have limited vision.
If you're completely blind, you have no vision.
If you're Congenital blind, This refers to poor vision that you are born with.

About Us

Let's Make A difference Today

Switzerland based philanthropist Jeffries Queye, in 2018, after haven realized the potency of the adage that says " disability means not inability" grabbed the bull by the horn and founded the MEB foundation which focus its charitable giving on advancing economic programmes for the blind and visually impared musicians in Ghana. Meb foundation is a registered and recognized non-profitable organization based in Ghana. The foundation mission is to use music as a tool to educate, entertain, empower, and inform people in Ghana and around Africa continent. Through a variety of sensational tunes, we enrich societies with an interculture understanding of extraordinary people learning to play instruments, thus transmitting a message for the need of inclusion in the world of workplaces for blind and visually impared musicians. Our vision is one where we percieve blind and visually impared individuals as musicians and teachers not as beggars or burden to society. We believe providing the rhythm and melody enables us to march together toward changing public opinions about Blindness and visual impairment.

Donation at Ghana National Basic Inclusive School,Cape Coast-Ghana

FROM LEFT: Benjamin Boateng (MD) - Meb Foundation, Phillanthropist Jeffries Quaye ( Exective Director) - Meb Foundation, Ernest Koku Esiwojo(Assistant Headmaster- Ghana National Basic Inclusive School,Cape Coast-Ghana)

On Sept 2nd,2021,the management team in the company of the founder and executive director, philanthropist Jeffries Quaye,who was on holidays from his resident country Switzerland to Ghana, visited two schools in Cape Coast in the Western Region.


The visit was part of the foundation's current nationwide donation of free braille reading materials to enhance the quality of library books for school for the visually impaired.


The visit and donations created a healthy opportunity for us to interact with partially blind students.


The schools that benefited from this free braille reading materials are Ghana National Basic Inclusive School-Cape Coast and Cape Coast School For The Deaf And Blind.


Receiving the free braille reading material,the assistant headmaster of Ghana National Basic Inclusive School,Cape Coast,Ernest Koku Esiwojo thanked Meb Foundation Ghana for the wonderful gesture and also assured that the Brailles will be put into good use.


They also asked for more


Meb Foundation is currently in a nationwide free braille distribution mood for the next 10 years.


With support from our US donor, who still insists to remain anonymous, Meb Foundation Ghana is currently embarking on a nationwide distribution of free braille reading materials --


The initiative is to enable the foundation's mission to ensure that basic reading materials reaches the majority of visually impaired across Ghana is achieved.


GHC 50,000 worth of braille is expected to be distributed across Ghana free of charge every month. Schools, individuals and institutions who are interested in our free braille reading materials can contact us.


Meb Foundation Ghana is determined to make a positive impact on the lives of visually impaired across Ghana-


At the Cape Coast School For The Blind And Deaf,supervisors and head mistress received the free bria with thanks giving and blessing.


Meb Foundation has plans to return to both schools with more free braille reading materials.

Borehole Pipe Project

Water is life and if its source is not properly managed life itself can turn against us in a deadly way.

Meb Foundation is extending its support hand to 4 villages in the Western Region to install borehole pipes for the deprived communities who have been hit with blindness due to poor drinking water.The project begins in October 2021 and expected to be completed in March 2022.
