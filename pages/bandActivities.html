Magic Eyes Band For All Your Events
Magic eyes band

Giving Hope Through Entertainment

Magic Eyes Band is an innovative tool of Meb Foundation,a non-governmental organization based in Ghana

The band is a group of talented blind musicians put together to help implement the vision of Meb Foundation

The band represent a dynamic symbol of the capabilities of the visually impaired.

The band is expected to raise funds through its performances both locally in Ghana and internationally

The Magic Eyes Band is honored to introduce Vitamin For The Soul Concert

Vitamin For The Soul Concert is a 2 hours non-stop gospel splash available for your church
